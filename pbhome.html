Welcome to Playboating.org

Welcome to Playboating.org is website will help you better understand the sport of freestyle kayaking. My intent is to help people that would like to get into the sport and progress into a paddler that can throw down on some freestyle tricks at your local lake, river or play spots. This website will be updated as I have time with dad duties and squeezing time in for boating keeps me busy.

How to use this site

Playboating.org will cover what is needed to get into the sport. Which can be found below in the Section Categories boxes most of the content will be video base links that I have found helpful and would like to share. I have broken the sections into three groups: New to the FreeStyle Kayaking, FreeStyle Tricks from easy to hard and gear links.
