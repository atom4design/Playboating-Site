What is Playboating?

Playboating is a style of Whitewater kayaking that focuses on freestyle and techin to perform tricks on flat water, waves, local playspot and holes on the river. A playboat will look much different than a normal kayak as it needs to fit the person using it pretty tight. This sport can be a great way to learn a new water skill set and get a great workout at the sametime. The progression of the sport is very similar to skateboarding, snowboarding and many other sport the have tricks to perform.
