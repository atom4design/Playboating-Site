Playboat Location and Ideas

Please read below for differnt place you can try playboating at around your area.

Whitewater Play Park

Traditional whitewater parks (often referred to as “Kayak Parks” or “Whitewater Kayak Parks”) are created using simple modifications to a natural river. These typically take the form of dam modifications, designed to allow for safe raft and kayak passage as well as recreational boating.

River Surf Waves

Standing waves are stationary on the river, caused by a high volume of water constricted by flowing over a rock and creating a wave behind. Kayak river surfing can face up-stream and catch waves and have the feeling of traveling fast over water while not actually moving.

Flat Water Lake

Flat water lakes are great to practice all playboating tricks and paddling technique. If your local area has a dock this can be used to launch off of the practice loops and other tricks.By putting in the lake time you will see real progression in your playboat style and opens the boating session up year round.
